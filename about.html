Who We Are

Ghana's premier media production company with a vision for excellence

Creating Visual Excellence Since 2015

YAC Media Productions was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to become Ghana's leading creative force in visual storytelling. What started as a small team of passionate filmmakers and photographers has grown into a full-service media production company serving clients across West Africa and beyond.

Our journey began when our founders recognized a gap in the market for high-quality, professional media production that could compete on an international level while maintaining authentic African storytelling. We've since worked with major brands, non-profits, government agencies, and individuals to bring their stories to life.

At YAC Media, we believe that every project tells a story worth sharing. Our approach combines technical expertise with creative vision, ensuring that each client receives a final product that not only meets but exceeds their expectations.
